+## Version 3.4.2
+
+_2016-11-03_
+
+ *  Fix: Recover gracefully when an HTTP/2 connection is shutdown. We had a
+    bug where shutdown HTTP/2 connections were considered usable. This caused
+    infinite loops when calls attempted to recover.
